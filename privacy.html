Website Privacy

Last Updated: March 30, 2026

1. Zero Trackers, Zero Cookies

Pi Studios is committed to absolute digital privacy. This website (pistudios.app) operates without the use of HTTP cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics scripts. We do not track your browsing behavior, and we do not collect, harvest, or sell personally identifiable information. Your visit to our site is entirely anonymous.

2. No Hidden Scripts

We believe in building clean, lightweight, and respectful software. Just as our macOS utilities contain zero bloatware, this website contains zero background surveillance. We do not use Google Analytics or Facebook Pixels.

3. App-Specific Privacy

Each application developed by Pi Studios operates under its own strict, application-specific Privacy Policy (such as the MacClip Privacy Policy). You can find those individual policies linked directly on their respective product pages or within the apps themselves.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Website Privacy Policy or our general data practices, please contact us at info@pistudios.app.
