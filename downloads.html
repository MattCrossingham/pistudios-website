Important Installation Note

Because these are pre-release test builds, they are not yet code-signed or notarized with an Apple Developer Account. macOS Gatekeeper will block them by default.

How to install:

  1. Unzip the downloaded file and drag the app into your Applications folder.
  2. Right-click (or hold Control and click) the app and select Open.
  3. When the warning appears, click Open anyway.

If macOS says the app is "damaged", open Terminal and run:
xattr -cr /Applications/MacClip.app
